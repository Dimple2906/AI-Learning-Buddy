If your AI Learning Buddy uses Streamlit and the Gemini API, this is usually enough:

streamlit
google-generativeai

If your app also uses python-dotenv to read environment variables (recommended for deployment), use:

streamlit
google-generativeai
python-dotenv

If you use pyngrok only in Google Colab, do not include it. Streamlit Community Cloud does not need ngrok.

Recommended requirements.txt
streamlit>=1.40.0
google-generativeai>=0.8.0
python-dotenv>=1.0.1
